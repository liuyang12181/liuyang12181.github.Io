Things to consider before you adopt

(It is very important!!)

1. When you adopt a pet, you must commit to caring for it for the rest of its life, which can be up to a decade or more.

2. How much time and space you have. Dogs and cats need exercise, play and companionship every day.

3. Food, veterinary care, pet licence and other supplies will be a consistent expense.

4. Don't abandon it once again. Give it enough care. Treat your pet like your family.

5. There are important information you should consider when adopting pets from overseas to ensure animals and humans are protected against diseases. Learn more about Adopting & Importing Pets from Overseas

What need to bring when you adopt a pet

-Anything that proves you (such as ID card, passport)

-Your personal information (such as address, telephone number)

At the time of your registration, you must sign a commitment to adopt a stray animal. When you have completed all the things, you can take your pet home!

How to welcome your new pet

-Prepare all necessary supplies for your dog or cat such as food, bowls, leash or harness, collar, brush, toys, scratch pad, bed.

-Don't put your household chemicals in places where pets can easily reach them, such as cleaners and pesticides.

-Don't let your pets touch dangerous items such as knives.

-If you have other pets at home. Please set up quarantine points for your new pet first.

-Keep doors closed including the doors to your washer and dryer, closets and cupboards.
